Make every claim count

Leveraging more than 15 years of experience with more than 2500 clinical audits and 650+ clients, Acorn and AYM provide programming integrity expertise that surpasses others in the industry. Our experience spans within the public sector e.g. the DOH, DHA and the MOH in UAE and the MOH in Saudi Arabia as well as with the private sector with hundreds of healthcare providers.

Our solution is bent on our long-held success in healthcare audits for the GCC market and the Abu Dhabi Health Authority. We have combined our local knowledge with the most efficient fraud prevention technologies and models used in the industry around the world, our AI solution helps payers detect erroneous or fraudulent claims — before reimbursing providers. Artificial intelligence (AI) models can be tailored to identify healthcare claims fraud, prescription abuse, up-charges, phantom billings, and many other FWA challenges. Our artificial intelligence (AI) platform can create prospective (pre-pay) models built by identifying anomalies found in historical claims data (post-pay).

Who it’s for:We provide a complementary AI solution that does not replace but integrates and improves outcomes for payment integrity providers, Payors and SIUs. Our technology uncovers complex schemes that otherwise is very difficult to detect with standard claim management systems

Amazing Features

Our technology creates an end-to-end suite of profiling and modeling capabilities that continuously adapt and improve results. This seamless combination of advanced AI tools delivers personalized decisions in seconds to payers, insurance companies, health authorities, payment processors and other payment integrity vendors.

Automation

An automated prioritizing “flagging” filter based on a rule based technology to identify high risk claims prior to payment. With increased detection of true FWA, insurers can focus their investigators’ time on highly likely fraud, waste or abuse.

Live Update

Since the technology enables live updates to existing models based on your real-time claims experience, our AI automates some of the manual processes involved in maintaining and updating existing rule-based technology.

Cost efficient

Evolving models scale with your data, increase detection rates, and decrease operational costs and false positives.

Analytics

Dashboards, analytics and reporting capabilities with custom reporting capabilities.

improve processes

The pre-payment model identifies FWA before the insurer sends good money for bad claims, significantly reducing the need for inefficient pay-and-chase activities.

Customized

Architected in collaboration with UAE Payors, Providers, Coders, Auditors, Developers and Data Scientists.

integration

Fully integrated solution with best-in-class user interface design for ease of use, rapid discovery, behavioral analytics, rules and supports of all claim types

Multi Resources

Aggregate data from multiple sources and not just claims data is analyzed - linear and nonlinear information to derive valuable insights. InCompliance analyses links between multiple providers.
