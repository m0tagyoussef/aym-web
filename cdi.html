  2. AYM-CDI®

Drive quality patient care and appropriate reimbursement

Our CDI solution combines computer-assisted clinical and physician documentation recommendations based on CMS documentation guidelines and coding crosswalks to ensure a service rendered is specific to a diagnosis thus improving patient outcomes while reducing denial rates when utilized. The solution was custom designed for the GCC market to help hospitals reduce duplicate work, improve and ensure supporting documentation is provided for services ordered, promote compliance, and ensure accurate reimbursement.

Outcome:Documentation accurately reflects patient diagnoses and procedures that are important to determining appropriate reimbursement. And specific evidence‑based documentation improves the process for managing denials.

Who it’s for:healthcare organizations looking to standardize coding and documentation, capture more reimbursements when applicable, ensure accurate reimbursements and fix a fragmented CDI process.

Amazing Features

Productivity is crucial and adding headcount may not be an option. With encounter prioritization, you can use the power of AI to focus on the cases that most affect quality and reimbursement. Your CDS will pinpoint opportunities faster, quickly find the clinical source of the evidence, and help you protect against denials. Encounter prioritization, automated workflows, intuitive navigation, built‑in documentation tips and decision trees and quick access to reference materials from ACDIS save time and let your team expand case coverage.

Focused Work-list

AI based classification of medical charts providing a prioritized work-list focused on opportunities to initiate clarification queries to the Physician.

Documentation Intel.

A full Documentation Intelligence library of Documentation tips for over 4000 CPT codes which guides clinical documentation specialists on query submissions to the Physician.

Decision Trees

A full library of Decision Trees to support the rendering of a specific CPT.

Medical Edits

An extensive and industry leading library of editable medical edits/physician queries to ensure compliance and consistency throughout your enterprise.

Interoperability

The ability to integrate with any EMR including Cerner, Epic, Meditech and other integration options utilizing standard HL7 messaging for our interfaces, allowing us to be “vendor agnostic” and resulting in faster implementations.

cost efficient

A proven track record with quick, cost efficient and seamless 30 to 40-day implementations.

Workflow

Streamlined workflow and communication tools that enable CDS, Coding staff and Physician Advisors to easily share information.

Managment

Compliance and management features that give insight into CDS productivity and physician query responsiveness at all levels of the facility, division and enterprise.
